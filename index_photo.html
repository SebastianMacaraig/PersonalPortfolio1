Lobo Batangas is one of our favorite destinations when it comes to outings and vacations, we've been going to lobo every weekend since i was 12 years old because the main focus of my fathers' business is centered there. Having the sound of the ocean in your background while enjoying freshly cooked Samgyupsal and sand on your feet is a havenly feeling worth the 2 hour long drive crossing two mountains just to reach this destination, Lobo is one of the municipalities of batangas which lies at the province's Southestern coast. Its bounderies are Batangas city on the west, the town of Taysan and Rosario on the north, San Juan on the east, and Verde Island Passage on the South. Lobo is also a third class municipality in the provice of Batangas.
